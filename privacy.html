Privacy policy

This overview explains how we treat information while keeping every play session entertainment-only.

Information collected

We keep account basics such as name, email, and optional display avatar. Analytics capture browser type, approximate region, and which comfort settings you toggle.

Usage

Data powers service emails, playlist curation, and aggregated reports about feature adoption. We never monetize personal info and only work with vendors who honor GDPR-level safeguards.

Cookies

Essential cookies maintain sessions and remember contrast selections. Analytics cookies help us understand engagement; you can disable them via the preference banner without losing access.

Rights

Adults can request access, correction, portability, or deletion by emailing privacy@luckyfortunerealm.com. Expect confirmation within two business days and resolution within thirty days.

Security & transfers

Infrastructure runs on encrypted hosting in the United States and the EU. If data crosses borders, we rely on standard contractual clauses.

Updates

We update this notice when regulations shift or new features launch. The effective date reflects the latest revision.
